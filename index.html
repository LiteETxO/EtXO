Ethiopia at the Forefront of Digital Infrastructure

The Process

How ETxO Works

From Ethiopia's clean hydropower to globally tradable Bitcoin — our end-to-end infrastructure model.

01

Stranded Energy Capture

Ethiopia's excess hydropower is converted into a globally liquid asset. We connect directly to energy infrastructure to source clean, low-cost power at scale.

02

Bitcoin Mining Operations

Our purpose-built data centers house industrial-grade mining equipment, operating 24/7 to generate Bitcoin with maximum efficiency and minimal environmental impact.

03

Value Creation & Reinvestment

Mining revenue is reinvested into expanding infrastructure, building local technical expertise, and strengthening Ethiopia's position in the global digital economy.

Infrastructure

Data Centers

ETxO's Digital Asset Mining initiative establishes state-of-the-art data centers in Ethiopia, designed for the mining of Bitcoin and digital assets. Our 10 MW pilot facility at the Ethiopian ICT Park is the foundation of a roadmap to 30 MW and beyond. These centers are not just hubs of digital innovation — they are powerful engines of economic development, driving revenue streams that support communities, build skills, and fund the next generation of digital infrastructure.

Looking ahead, ETxO's infrastructure is designed to evolve into next-generation data centers supporting high-performance computing and AI workloads — positioning Ethiopia as a global hub for advanced computing.
